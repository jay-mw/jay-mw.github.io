Chicago At The Exposition: The Midwestern Spectacle

09.26.2020

Technologies of Capture and the Mail-Order Catalogue

Jay is an artist, writer, and loser from the Midwest. They live in Chicago, where they make works about the Great Lakes, pigeons, and capitalist accumulation while challenging historical narratives through collage. With the pretentious art-speak out of the way, they mostly enjoy reading about the Midwest and exploring archives and letting those interests meld with the critical theory brain rot decaying their brain.

Jay does not hold an MFA and does not wish to in the near future. Maybe there is an MLIS in the cards for them.

They have yet to appear in any publications.

Their twitter, facebook, tumblr, and such are none of your business.

Their linkedin should only be visited as a last resort.
