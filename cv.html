Writing

A Commonplace Book of Grackles
Poetry. In HOMINTERN. Capitalist accumulation; the abolition of space; annoying birds; agriculture and its discontents.
Passenger Pigeons: The Apparatus of Memory
Essay. In baest. Memory and loss; fruitless econostalgia; everything is indelicately interconnected; the logic of capital; Chicago and the taming of the Middle and Great Wests.
Glacial Tillage: On Erasure and the Midwest
Essay. In BELT Magazine. Travelling to Copper County; erased narratives; covert racism; the political economy of the midwest.
